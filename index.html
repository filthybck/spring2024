How did you become interested in Web Design? I have had a certain insatiable obsession with computers and the internet in general since the days of using dial-up to get on AOL, and looking at the source code of HTML (I think I managed the right terminology here!) has intrigued me as much as confused me even so long ago, at such a young age. I remember a certain website that was sort of a simplified way of making your own page, I want to say it was maybe called something like angelfire? Anywho, I remember attempting at putting together a page without much direction even way back then, which then eventually evolved into my Myspace profile as that was the big thing at that point in time, which if I am not mistaken, incorporated HTML into formatting your customizable page(utilizing the all powerful copy+paste to harness someone elses code, if I remember correctly) Now here we are in 2024, and I am stoked to hopefully be able to officially learn some of the magic behind real web design!
